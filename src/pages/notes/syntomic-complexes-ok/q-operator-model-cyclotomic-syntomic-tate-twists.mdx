## Abstract

Let $p$ be an odd prime and $R=\mathbb{Z}_p[\zeta_p]$. We identify
$\mathbb{Z}_p(i)(R)$, for $i \geq 1$, with the total fiber of an explicit
commuting square of $q$-operators on $A=\mathbb{Z}_p[[q-1]]$. The comparison
applies Liu's one-correspondence construction to the defining Nygaard pullback
and removes the Hodge-completed derived de Rham term by octahedral
cancellation. A finite Herr reduction and an integral cyclotomic residue then
compute the cohomology without using the syntomic-etale comparison.

## Abstract

Let $K/\mathbb{Q}_p$ be a finite extension with ring of integers
$\mathcal{O}_K$. We compute the BMS syntomic complexes

$$
\mathbb{Z}_p(i)(\mathcal{O}_K)
  =
\mathrm{R}\Gamma_{\mathrm{syn}}(\mathrm{Spec}\,\mathcal{O}_K,\mathbb{Z}_p(i))
$$

for all integer weights $i \in \mathbb{Z}$. For $i < 0$ the complexes vanish.
For $i \geq 0$ the calculation reduces, through the comparison of Bhatt--Mathew
with Geisser--Sato--Schneider $p$-adic etale Tate twists, to local Galois
cohomology.

The answer is expressed in terms of the completed unit group, the rank
$[K:\mathbb{Q}_p]$, and the finite cyclotomic torsion groups

$$
W_m(K)=H^0(K,\mathbb{Q}_p/\mathbb{Z}_p(m)).
$$

The note also gives a complete calculation of $W_m(K)$ from the image of the
$p$-adic cyclotomic character.

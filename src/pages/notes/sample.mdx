This placeholder note verifies that notes can be written in MDX and can include
inline mathematics such as $E_2^{p,q}$ or $\pi_n(X)$.

Displayed mathematics is also supported:

$$
E_2^{p,q} \Rightarrow H^{p+q}(X)
$$

Future lecture notes, seminar notes, and reading notes can be added as new
`.mdx` files in `src/pages/notes/`.
